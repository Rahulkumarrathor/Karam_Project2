ABOUT KARAM

KARAM is India's leading Personal protective Equipment Manufacturing enterprise,andis rated as one of the finest India companies providing world class PPE. Our product range include personal protection Equipment like safety helmets, safety eyewear, hearing protective equlpment,welding protective face shields,respiratory protective equipment,safety shoes,and avast range of fall Protection Equipment.
